FAQ

Questions, answered

What investors, partners, and drug developers most often ask us.

What exactly does Bizon Labs' technology do?

We precisely tune the critical quality attributes of lipid nanoparticles, particle size and morphology, giving drug developers direct, reproducible control over how LNPs form, from microfluidic to pilot scale.

How is this different from standard LNP formulation?

Conventional workflows offer limited control over the formation process itself. Our platform maps process parameters to particle attributes, so you can target a specific size (40–450 nm) and morphology on demand, and predict the outcome before running a batch.

Who is the technology for?

Teams developing mRNA therapeutics and vaccines, gene editing and cell therapies, rare-disease treatments, and self-amplifying RNA. In short, anywhere reproducible, high-performing LNPs matter.

How can we partner or invest?

We're actively speaking with potential pharma partners and investors. Reach out through the form below or email office@bizonlabs.com.
